## What is an EWTN Media Missionary?

An EWTN Media Missionary is a volunteer who supports and promotes EWTN's mission by sharing the Gospel through media. Media Missionaries play a vital role in spreading the Eternal Word and teaching others that Jesus Christ is the Way, the Truth, and the Life.

---

## How to Become an EWTN Media Missionary

### Step 1: Register Online

1. **Visit**: https://www.ewtnmissionaries.com/signup
2. **Select**: Your country and state/province
3. **Provide**: Contact information
4. **Submit**: Registration form

### Step 2: Receive Starter Pack

Upon registration, you'll receive:
- **Starter Pack** by mail with resources for missionary activities
- **Seasonal network highlights**
- **Bi-monthly emails** with updates and resources
- **Quarterly newsletter** with seasonal posters to share

**All resources are complimentary!**

### Step 3: Contact Information

- **Phone**: 205-795-5771
- **Email**: ewtnmissionaries@ewtn.com
- **Website**: https://www.ewtnmissionaries.com
