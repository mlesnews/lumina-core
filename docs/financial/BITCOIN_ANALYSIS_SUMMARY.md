## Key Findings

### ✅ Verified Claims (8)

1. **Inflation as Hidden Tax** - VERIFIED
   - Inflation systematically erodes purchasing power
   - Average 2-3% annual inflation = ~85% loss since 1971

2. **1971 Fiat Transition** - VERIFIED
   - Nixon ended gold convertibility on August 15, 1971
   - Shifted from asset-backed to trust-based monetary system

3. **21 Million Supply Cap** - VERIFIED
   - Bitcoin protocol enforces hard cap of 21 million BTC
   - ~19.7 million mined, final coin estimated 2140

4. **Decentralization** - VERIFIED
   - ~15,000+ nodes globally
   - Censorship-resistant transactions
   - Parallel to our air gap architecture

5. **Cantillon Effect** - VERIFIED
   - Well-established economic concept
   - Wealth inequality increased since 1971
   - Bitcoin's fixed issuance mitigates this

6. **Volatility** - VERIFIED WITH CONTEXT
   - 60-80% annualized volatility
   - Decreases as market matures
   - Similar to early Amazon stock

7. **Energy Consumption** - VERIFIED WITH NUANCE
   - ~150 TWh/year (~0.5% global)
   - ~50%+ renewable energy
   - Comparable to traditional banking

8. **Lightning Network** - VERIFIED
   - Enables instant, low-cost transactions
   - ~5,000+ BTC locked
   - Growing adoption

### ❌ Debunked Claims (2)

1. **Crime Narrative** - DEBUNKED
   - <1% of Bitcoin transactions are illicit
   - USD preferred for crime (cash is untraceable)
   - Bitcoin's transparency aids law enforcement

2. **Ponzi Scheme** - DEBUNKED
   - No central operator
   - No promise of returns
   - Decentralized protocol, not investment scheme
