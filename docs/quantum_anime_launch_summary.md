## Pilot Episode Breakdown

### Scene Structure (20 minutes content)

1. **Opening: The Flat World** (2 min)
   - Alex wakes as a tiny dot in 1D space
   - Educational: Understanding one-dimensional space
   - Spacefaring: Linear motion in space travel

2. **Discovery: Meeting Another Dot** (3 min)
   - Alex meets another dot, learns about relationships
   - Educational: Relationships in 1D space
   - Spacefaring: Single-axis navigation

3. **Realization: There Must Be More** (2.5 min)
   - Alex senses compression, feels missing dimensions
   - Educational: Recognizing limitations leads to discovery
   - Spacefaring: Foundation for multi-dimensional navigation

4. **The Second Dimension** (3 min)
   - Alex discovers up/down movement
   - Educational: Understanding 2D space, longitude/latitude
   - Spacefaring: 2D navigation, mapping, coordinates

5. **Mastering Two Dimensions** (2.5 min)
   - Alex learns to navigate and map in 2D
   - Educational: Mastering coordinate systems
   - Spacefaring: Planetary surface navigation

6. **The Third Dimension Beckons** (2 min)
   - Alex senses the third dimension, reaches upward
   - Educational: Preview of 3D space
   - Spacefaring: Foundation for 3D space navigation

7. **Closing: Next Time** (1 min)
   - Preview of next episode, tagline, logo
