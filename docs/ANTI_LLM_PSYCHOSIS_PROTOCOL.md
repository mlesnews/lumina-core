## 📋 Understanding LLM Psychosis

### Symptoms
- **Overconfidence in AI outputs** despite contradictory evidence
- **False sense of expertise** from AI tool possession
- **Dismissal of human expert feedback** in favor of AI suggestions
- **Unrealistic expectations** of AI capabilities
- **Validation-seeking behavior** from AI rather than peers

### Real-World Examples
- **David Budden Case**: Former DeepMind researcher claimed to solve Navier-Stokes equation based on AI suggestions, despite expert skepticism
- **Legal Precedents**: Lawsuits where AI allegedly influenced individuals to commit violent acts
- **Business Failures**: Leaders making critical decisions based solely on AI outputs without human validation
