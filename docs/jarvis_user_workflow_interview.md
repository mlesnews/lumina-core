# JARVIS User Workflow Interview

**Purpose:** Understand YOUR actual workflow, patterns, and needs
**Approach:** Design WITH you, not FOR you
**Goal:** Create symbiotic JARVIS that becomes part of you

---

## 🎯 **THE QUESTIONS**

### **1. Your Daily Workflow**

**How do you actually work?**
- What's your typical day?
- What tasks do you do most?
- What's your process?
- What's your mental model?

**What would help us understand:**
- Walkthrough of a typical day
- Your actual workflow (not theoretical)
- What you actually do (not what you think you should do)

---

### **2. Your Patterns & Preferences**

**What are your patterns?**
- How do you think about tasks?
- What's your organization method?
- What feels natural to you?
- What's your muscle memory?

**What would help us understand:**
- Your actual patterns (not ideal patterns)
- What you actually do (not what you think you should do)
- Your preferences (not generic preferences)

---

### **3. Your Interaction Style**

**How do you want to interact with JARVIS?**
- Voice commands? (What phrases feel natural?)
- Keyboard shortcuts? (Which keys feel right?)
- Both? (When to use which?)
- Something else?

**What would help us understand:**
- Your actual interaction style
- What feels natural to YOU
- What would be perfect for YOU

---

### **4. Your Pain Points**

**What frustrates you?**
- What breaks your flow?
- What slows you down?
- What feels wrong?
- What's missing?

**What would help us understand:**
- Your actual pain points (not theoretical)
- What actually frustrates you (not what should)
- What would actually solve your problems

---

### **5. Your Vision**

**What's your vision for JARVIS?**
- What should it be?
- How should it feel?
- What's the ideal?
- What would be perfect?

**What would help us understand:**
- Your actual vision (not generic vision)
- What would feel magical to YOU
- What would make you say "this is it"

---

## 🎤 **YOUR RESPONSES**

**Please share:**
1. Your actual workflow

3. Your interaction style
4. Your pain points
5. Your vision

**We'll use this to:**
- Design WITH you
