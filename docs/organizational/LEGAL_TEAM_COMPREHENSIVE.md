## 📋 Legal Processes

### Contract Review Process

1. **Contract Receipt** → Receive contract for review
2. **Risk Assessment** → Assess legal risk (Decision Tree + Risk Matrix)
3. **Legal Review** → Review contract terms
4. **Negotiation** → Negotiate terms (if needed)
5. **Approval** → Get legal approval
6. **Execution** → Execute contract
7. **Management** → Manage contract lifecycle

### Compliance Process

1. **Compliance Assessment** → Assess compliance requirements
2. **Compliance Planning** → Plan compliance activities
3. **Compliance Implementation** → Implement compliance measures
4. **Compliance Monitoring** → Monitor compliance
5. **Compliance Reporting** → Report compliance status
6. **Compliance Improvement** → Improve compliance

### Risk Assessment Process

1. **Legal Risk Identification** → Identify legal risks
2. **Risk Assessment** → Assess risk (Decision Tree + Risk Matrix)
3. **Risk Mitigation** → Mitigate risks
4. **Risk Monitoring** → Monitor risks
5. **Risk Reporting** → Report risks
6. **Risk Improvement** → Improve risk management
