### Episode Structure

**40-Minute Episodes:**
- **20 minutes** - Educational content
- **20 minutes** - Marketing/commercials
  - 10 blocks of 2 minutes each
  - Scattered throughout content at strategic intervals
  - Types: Commercials, Sponsors, Educational, Merchandise, Next Episode Previews, Behind Scenes, Interactive

### Marketing Block Strategy

**10 Marketing Blocks Per Episode (2 minutes each):**

1. **5% mark** - Next Episode Preview (hook viewers)
2. **12% mark** - Commercial (product/service)
3. **20% mark** - Educational Content Marketing
4. **28% mark** - Sponsor Message
5. **35% mark** - Merchandise Promotion
6. **45% mark** - Commercial
7. **55% mark** - Behind the Scenes
8. **65% mark** - Interactive Element (QR codes, etc.)
9. **75% mark** - Educational Content
10. **90% mark** - Next Episode Preview (retention)

### Style Progression

**Season 1-2:** Saturday Morning 80s/90s
- References: He-Man, ThunderCats, Transformers, X-Men
- Style: Bold lines, high contrast, heroic design
- Target: Ages 5-11

**Season 3-6:** Cartoon Network
- References: Adventure Time, Steven Universe, Regular Show
- Style: Squash and stretch, simplified expressive design
- Target: Ages 8-14

**Season 7-12:** Crunchyroll Anime
- References: Demon Slayer, Attack on Titan, My Hero Academia
- Style: Detailed smooth animation, atmospheric backgrounds
- Target: Ages 12+
