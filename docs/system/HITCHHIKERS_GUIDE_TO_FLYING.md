## The Secret to Flying

**"There is an art to flying, or rather a way of flying. The knack lies in learning how to throw yourself at the ground and miss."**

### The Concept

The secret to flying is:
1. **Throw yourself at the ground**
2. **Miss**

The problem is that most people, at the critical moment, **think about falling** - and then they fall.
