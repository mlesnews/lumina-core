## SYPHON vs Siphon

### SYPHON (Correct Spelling)

**SYPHON** is the correct spelling and is the **name** of the intelligence extraction system.

**SYPHON** is **NOT an acronym**. It is simply the name chosen for the system, evoking the concept of "siphoning" or extracting intelligence from various sources.

### What SYPHON Is

**SYPHON** is a:
- **Standardized** system
- **Modular** architecture
- **Self-Healing** capabilities
- Intelligence **Extraction** system

But "SYPHON" itself is not an acronym - it's the system name. The description "Standardized, Modular, Self-Healing Intelligence Extraction System" describes what SYPHON does, but those words don't spell out SYPHON.

### Why Not Use an Acronym?

Rather than forcing a backronym (backwards acronym) that doesn't actually spell the word, **SYPHON** is simply the system name. This is cleaner and more honest than creating a fake acronym.

### Siphon (Incorrect Spelling)

**Siphon** (lowercase 's') refers to:
- A tube used to convey liquid from a container
- To draw off or transfer (liquid)

This is **NOT** what we're referring to.
