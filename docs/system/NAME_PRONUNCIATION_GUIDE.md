# Name Pronunciation Guide - Lesnewski

## Correct Spelling
**L-E-S-N-E-W-S-K-I**

## Pronunciation
**"Less-New-Ski"** (all one word)

## Phonetic Transcription
**/lɛs-nu-ski/**

---

## Breakdown

### Les (First Syllable)
- **Word**: "less" (opposite of more)
- **Phonetic**: /lɛs/
- **Speech Pathologist Notes**: Short 'e' sound, like in 'let' or 'bet'
- **How to say**: Say "less" as in "less than"

### New (Second Syllable)
- **Word**: "new" (opposite of old)
- **Phonetic**: /nu/
- **Speech Pathologist Notes**: Long 'u' sound, like in 'blue' or 'true'
- **How to say**: Say "new" as in "new car"

### Ski (Third Syllable)
- **Word**: "ski" (like skiing)
- **Phonetic**: /ski/
- **Speech Pathologist Notes**: Hard 'sk' sound followed by long 'e', like in 'skiing'
- **How to say**: Say "ski" as in "skiing"

**Say**: "Less" (as in "less than")
