## What Are Double Ontandras?

### Definition
- **Double Entendres** - A figure of speech with double meaning
- **Primary Meaning** - The obvious, surface meaning
- **Secondary Meaning** - The deeper, alternative meaning
- **Double Layer** - Both meanings simultaneously

### In Our Context

**Examples from our conversation**:

1. **"Break a Leg"**
   - **Primary**: Good luck (theatrical expression)
   - **Secondary**: Actually break a leg (if needed)
   - **Double**: Figuratively and/or literally

2. **"Hope You Miss"**
   - **Primary**: Hope you succeed
   - **Secondary**: When throwing yourself at ground, miss it (fly)
   - **Double**: Success and flying

3. **"Bird is the Word"**
   - **Primary**: BIRD is the key concept
   - **Secondary**: Not Design - BIRD IS THE WORD
   - **Double**: BIRD vs Design distinction

4. **"Make It So"**
   - **Primary**: Execute the command
   - **Secondary**: Make it happen, actually do it
   - **Double**: Action, not just planning
