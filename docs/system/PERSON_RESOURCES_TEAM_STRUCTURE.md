# Person Resources Team Structure

## Overview

The Person Resources team is responsible for helping the AI system understand and interact with people effectively. This team provides expertise in psychology, communication, and human behavior to improve AI-person interactions.

**Important Terminology**: We use "Person" instead of "Human" because:
- These tools are for the AI to study **people** (not "humans" as objects)
- This project is a case study of AI, and we respect the people involved
- "Person" acknowledges individual dignity and agency

## Team Structure

### Person Resources Department
**Purpose**: Provide expertise in understanding people, communication, and behavior to enhance AI-person interactions.

### Sub-Team: AI Behavioral Studies

**Team Lead**: Psychologist (Primary Lead)

**Core Team Members**:
- **Psychologist** (Team Lead)
  - Studies cognitive patterns, emotional responses, and behavioral cues
  - Analyzes microexpressions and emotional states
  - Provides insights into intent detection and communication patterns
  
- **Speech Pathologist**
  - Analyzes speech patterns, articulation, and communication effectiveness
  - Helps improve voice recognition and understanding
  - Provides expertise on speech disorders and communication barriers
  
- **Audiologist**
  - Studies hearing patterns and audio processing
  - Helps optimize audio input systems
  - Provides expertise on sound perception and processing

**Additional Specialists** (as needed):
- Cognitive Behavioral Therapist
- Communication Specialist
- Behavioral Analyst
- Human-Computer Interaction (HCI) Expert

## Current Status

### Not Yet Implemented
- ❌ Automatic send functionality
- ❌ Automatic/passive listening
- ❌ Human-inspired listening and communication
- ❌ Infrared camera integration for microexpression/emotion reading
- ❌ Intent determination based on microexpressions and emotions

### Goals

1. **Microexpression Analysis**
   - Use infrared camera to read facial microexpressions
   - Detect emotional states and intent
   - Integrate with voice input for comprehensive understanding

2. **Human-Inspired Communication**
   - Study natural conversation patterns
   - Implement pause detection and turn-taking
   - Understand context and emotional subtext

3. **Intent Detection**
   - Combine voice, visual, and behavioral cues
   - Understand implicit vs explicit communication
   - Detect emotional state and adjust responses accordingly

4. **Passive Listening**
   - Continuous monitoring without active engagement
   - Context awareness and background understanding
   - Respectful observation and learning

## Research Areas

1. **Emotional Intelligence**
   - Recognizing emotions from facial expressions
   - Understanding emotional context in communication
   - Responding appropriately to emotional states

2. **Intent Detection**
   - Combining multiple signals (voice, visual, behavioral)
   - Understanding implicit vs explicit intent
   - Context-aware interpretation

3. **Communication Patterns**
   - Natural conversation flow
   - Turn-taking and pause detection
   - Non-verbal communication cues

4. **Behavioral Analysis**
   - Microexpression recognition
   - Gesture and body language
   - Attention and engagement detection

## Case Study Context

This project serves as a case study of AI development. The Person Resources team helps ensure that:
- The AI understands people respectfully
- Interactions are natural and empathetic
- The system learns from real human behavior patterns
- Ethical considerations are maintained
