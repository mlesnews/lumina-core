## 🔗 Liaison: Lines of Business ↔ Technical Support

The **Project Management Team** is the **liaison between Lines of Business (LOB)** and **Technical Support** (Problem Management, Change Management, Helpdesk).

| From | To | PM role |
|------|-----|---------|
| **Lines of Business** | Technical Support | Translate LOB needs, priorities, and constraints into requests, RFCs, and prioritization for Problem/Change Management and Helpdesk. |
| **Technical Support** | Lines of Business | Communicate status, risks, change windows, and resolution outcomes to LOB; escalate when business impact or threat requires LOB input. |

**Responsibilities**:
- Represent LOB interests in CAB, problem reviews, and change scheduling.
- Represent Technical Support (capacity, risk, timelines) to LOB.
- Ensure Threat + Customer-Impact (and @TRIAGE / @BAU) are aligned with LOB priorities.
- Single point of coordination for cross-team requests (LOB → Helpdesk, Helpdesk → LOB).

**References**: [HELPDESK_PROBLEM_CHANGE_MANAGEMENT_STRUCTURE.md](../HELPDESK_PROBLEM_CHANGE_MANAGEMENT_STRUCTURE.md), [CHANGE_MANAGEMENT_REVIEW_CA_PA_IDE_JARVIS_CHAT_SEGMENT.md](../CHANGE_MANAGEMENT_REVIEW_CA_PA_IDE_JARVIS_CHAT_SEGMENT.md) (Threat + Customer-Impact, @TRIAGE @BAU).
