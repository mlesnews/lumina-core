# Jedi Temple Training Program

**The Official LUMINA Education Program - Become a LUMINA Jedi**

---

## 🎯 Mission

**Goal**: Teach the public how to use LUMINA to its fullest extent, making it easy and easiest for the next human.

**Approach**: Progressive, gamified training from Youngling to Master, with A/B testing to find optimal teaching methods.

---

## 🎓 Training Ranks

### 1. Youngling (Beginner)
- **The Force Awakens**: Introduction to LUMINA
- **Voice Coding Basics**: Learn to code with your voice

**Advancement**: Complete 2 Youngling modules → Padawan

### 2. Padawan (Intermediate)
- **The Way of Workflows**: Master LUMINA workflows

**Advancement**: Complete Padawan modules → Knight

### 3. Knight (Advanced)
- **Advanced Techniques**: Advanced LUMINA mastery

**Advancement**: Complete Knight modules → Master

### 4. Master (Expert)
- **Mastery of the Force**: Become a LUMINA Master

**Achievement**: Complete all modules and master certification

## 🧪 A/B Testing

### Test Design

**Method A: Visual + Interactive**
- 8-year-old friendly
- Visual demonstrations
- Hands-on practice
- Gamified learning

**Method B: Explanatory + Step-by-step**
- Traditional teaching
- Detailed explanations
- Text-based tutorials
- Structured progression

### Metrics

1. **Completion Time**: How long to complete training
2. **Success Rate**: Percentage who complete successfully
3. **Satisfaction Score**: User satisfaction (0-1)
4. **Knowledge Retention**: How well concepts are retained
5. **Jedi Rank Achieved**: Highest rank reached

### Random Assignment

Participants are randomly assigned to Method A or B (50/50 split) to ensure fair comparison.
