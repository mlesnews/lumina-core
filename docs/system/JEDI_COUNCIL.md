# Jedi Council - Upper Management Approval Board

## Overview

"THIS IS OUR JEDI COUNCIL, OUR UPPER MANAGEMENT APPROVAL BOARD OF AI #REASONING #DECISIONING #TROUBLESHOOTING[#PROBLEM-SOLVING]."

The Jedi Council is the upper management approval board for all AI reasoning, decision-making, troubleshooting, and problem-solving. All council members must vote before decisions are made or systems are deployed.

## Deliberation Process

1. **Question Submitted**: Question is submitted to the council
2. **Category Assigned**: Question is categorized (reasoning, decisioning, troubleshooting, problem-solving)
3. **Council Votes**: All active council members vote
4. **Consensus Determined**: Final status is determined from votes
5. **Action Items Extracted**: Conditions and optimizations are extracted
6. **Decision Recorded**: Decision is saved for future reference

---

## Approval Status

### APPROVED
- All council members approve
- No concerns or conditions
- Proceed with confidence

### CONDITIONAL
- Approved with conditions
- Some concerns or optimizations needed
- Proceed with caution and monitor outcomes

### REJECTED
- Council rejects
- Concerns must be addressed
- Do not proceed

### PENDING
- Deliberation in progress
- Awaiting all votes

---

## Consensus Threshold

- **100% Approval**: Fully approved
- **70%+ Approval**: Approved (majority)
- **<70% Approval**: Conditional (requires conditions)
- **Any Rejection**: Rejected (must address concerns)
