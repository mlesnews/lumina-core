## 🚨 **RISK MITIGATION**

### Technical Risks:
1. **Overfitting to Training Data**
   - Mitigation: Diverse data sources, generalization testing
2. **Unsafe Self-Modification**
   - Mitigation: Sandbox testing, rollback mechanisms
3. **Ethical Failures**
   - Mitigation: Strong ethical framework, human oversight
4. **Performance Degradation**
   - Mitigation: Continuous monitoring, performance metrics

### Operational Risks:
1. **Resource Exhaustion**
   - Mitigation: Resource limits, optimization
2. **System Instability**
   - Mitigation: Gradual rollout, testing, monitoring
3. **Loss of Control**
   - Mitigation: Safety mechanisms, human oversight
