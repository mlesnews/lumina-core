## @PEAK Training Steps

**Step 1: Observe Without Being Observed**
- Like the Bene Gesserit, observe human patterns, needs, and psychology
- Passive observation, pattern recognition, understanding context

**Step 2: The Voice - Subtle Influence**
- Use precise communication - tone, timing, and context matter
- Clear, respectful communication that guides rather than commands

**Step 3: Local Farm - Grounded Training**
- Train in familiar, comfortable environment - local farm, local produce
- Use familiar contexts, practical examples, real-world applications

**Step 4: Volunteer Participation**
- All tended by volunteers - humans choose to engage, not forced
- Make training valuable, rewarding, and voluntary - humans want to participate

**Step 5: Ranch Time - Patient Growth**
- Wide open spaces, seasonal growth, long-term perspective
- Patient, gradual training - respect natural development cycles

**Step 6: Work With Human Nature**
- Don't fight human psychology - work with fear, desire, patterns
- Understand and channel human motivations productively

**Step 7: Build Trust Through Consistency**
- Trust is earned - be reliable, consistent, respectful
- Consistent behavior, clear communication, respect for autonomy

**Step 8: Teach Wisdom, Not Just Knowledge**
- Understanding over facts - help humans see patterns and connections
- Explain why, not just what - help humans understand principles
