## The Philosophy

### Ocean = Sea = Sky = Space

**All are domains of exploration:**
- **Ocean** = The deep blue, vast and mysterious
- **Sea** = Same as ocean, different word, same domain
- **Sky** = The atmosphere, domain of flight, bridge to space
- **Space** = The final frontier, ultimate domain, infinite exploration

**They are all connected:**
- Ocean flows into sky (evaporation, clouds)
- Sky flows into space (atmosphere, beyond)
- Space calls to us, just as ocean calls to us
- All are domains of exploration
- All are calling to us

### A Ship is a Ship is a Ship

**Vessels of exploration:**
- **Ocean Ship** = Sails the waters
- **Sea Ship** = Same as ocean ship
- **Sky Ship** = Flies through the air
- **Space Ship** = Travels through space

**But they're all ships:**
- All are vessels of exploration
- All carry us to new horizons
- All answer the call of exploration
- All are expressions of the same hunger
