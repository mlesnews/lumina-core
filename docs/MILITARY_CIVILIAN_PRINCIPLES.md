## ⚖️ The Principles

### Air Force / Military

> **"The Air Force has a policy enforced by the UCMJ, and civilians have their own judicial system.**
>
> **Break a law while enlisted in the military, and you get to play 'double jeopardy'**
>
> **and learn that 'ignorance is not an excuse.'"**

#### 1. UCMJ Enforcement

- **Uniform Code of Military Justice** enforced
- **Military judicial system** applies
- **Strict enforcement** of rules and regulations

#### 2. Double Jeopardy

- **Break a law while enlisted** → Face **BOTH** military AND civilian consequences
- **No protection** from double jeopardy
- **Punishment in both systems**

#### 3. Ignorance is Not an Excuse

- **Not knowing the law/rules** is **NOT** a valid defense
- **You are responsible** for knowing the rules
- **Punishment regardless** of knowledge

---

### Post-Military (Banking & IT)

> **"Perception drives reality."**

#### 1. Perception Drives Reality

- **How things are perceived** is more important than how they actually are
- **What people believe** becomes reality
- **Reputation and trust** are everything

#### 2. Reality Follows Perception

- **Market forces** respond to perception
- **Customer trust** based on perception
- **Business outcomes** determined by perception
